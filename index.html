Our Story

Honoring the legacy of Peter Festus (1943–2018)

PF Place is a community-centered care facility created in loving memory of Peter Festus (PF). His life was dedicated to serving others through the church and supporting the elderly as well as individuals with mental and physical challenges.

Inspired by his compassion and commitment, PF Place continues his legacy of care, dignity, and community support. Conveniently located at 537 Old Point Avenue, Hampton, VA 23663.

Continuing a legacy of love — one resident at a time.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes PF Place different?
Our S.C.R.I.P.T. values guide every interaction: Safety, Clients, Respect, Integrity, Passion, Trust. We focus on personalized care and dignified living.
What types of services do you offer?
Transportation, medication management, nutrition, fitness, recreation, companionship, emotional support — 24/7 care tailored to individual needs.
What are the admission requirements?
Residents must be 18+, require assisted living support, able to receive help from trained staff, and meet financial/documentation requirements.
Can I schedule a tour?
Absolutely! Call (804) 514-8117 or email festosplace@gmail.com to arrange a personal tour.
Do you accept Medicare/Medicaid?
We work with various long‑term care insurance plans and private pay options.
